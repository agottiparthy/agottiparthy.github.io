We worked with the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art to redesign their ground floors from a lobby to a community space. Our team worked with SFMoMA's Board of Directors. See more at their website.


Part 1: Human Centered Design

Turn the first floors of SFMoMA into a vibrant community hub using design principles.


Approach

  • Took a deep dive into the most successful "third places" around the US to understand and utilize design trends.
  • Conducted ethnographic interviews of 50-plus museum goers to build a segmentation model and visitor journey maps.
  • Optimized user experience by identifying friction points and translating solutions into designs and floor plans.

Part 2: Marketing Strategy

Create marketing campaigns that will make SFMoMA as popular third place in SF.


Approach

  • Created a segmentation model for SFMoMA visitors through ethnographic interviews and surveys.
  • Designed marketing campaigns by defining target audiences, audience needs, and desired responses.
  • Prototyped marketing campaigns and validated recommendations by A/B testing on focus groups.
