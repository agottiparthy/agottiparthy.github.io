About Me


I'm passionate about building products that bring joy to users and solve real problems. From augmented reality to widespread 5G to advanced AI, a range of technologies coming to market in the next decade will define what it means to be a young person in society. I'm a strong advocate for spreading the riches of modern society by increasing access to knowledge, technology, and resources. I've worked to make this vision a reality by working I'm passionate about making a tech junky that's passionate about building and launching the products of tomorrow. I've worked with some of my favorite companies across emerging industries on projects related to growth strategy, GTM strategy, and UX optimization. I'm also a rising junior at UC Berkeley majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Statistics. In my free time I like to cook, read, play video games, and spend time with friends and family.

I believe technology's greatest potential lies in its ability to improve the lives of users, so I'm always looking for opportunities to work with teams in any of my areas of interest. In the long term, I hope to bring the skills and experience that comes with working in cross-functional roles on product teams in industry to the non-profit or social impact sphere. I'm always looking to meet new people so feel free to contact me at gottiparthy.ani@berkeley.edu or any of the social media platforms I'm active on.
